About Me

Over the course of my journey into 3D art, I have developed a deep passion and understanding of 3D content creation. It all began with 3D modeling and then i took a deep dive into all aspects computer graphics both technically and artistically. My drive has taken me to a point where I can use my abilities in the pipeline by pushing a vision to its completion and beyond, and also building tools that help other team members stream-line the production process. My main passion lies in creating photorealistic visuals and stylized visuals, but i also enjoy solving problems with programming or scripting. I am a self-taught artist and programmer driven by pure ardor.
